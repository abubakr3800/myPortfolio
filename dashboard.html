Social Media Links

Add your social media links to help visitors connect with you. You can use Linktree to collect all your links in one place, or add individual social media links.

Linktree Option (Recommended)

Linktree allows you to collect all your social media links in one place. This is perfect if you have multiple social accounts.

Enter your complete Linktree URL
How to create a Linktree:
  1. Go to linktr.ee
  2. Sign up for a free account
  3. Add your social media links
  4. Copy your Linktree URL and paste it above
